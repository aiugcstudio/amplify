The 3 bottlenecks between your framework and a full book of engagements.

Your framework is the product. Finding the right companies, slow proposal turnaround, and reporting drag are the three constraints in the way.

Bottleneck 1

Finding the right companies at the right inflection point

Growth-stage companies between $1M and $100M are your sweet spot, but identifying which ones are hitting the revenue or ops ceiling right now, and reaching them before a competitor does, takes hours of manual research every week.

The math: if you spend 8 to 10 hours a week on prospecting and research to maintain a pipeline, that is 40+ hours a month not spent on billable work. At $10K/month per engagement, that is effectively one engagement's worth of revenue lost to business development.
Bottleneck 2

Proposal and scoping turnaround slowing the close

Every prospect needs a custom proposal that maps their specific bottlenecks to your Evaluate, Establish, Engage framework. That scoping work takes time, and the longer between the discovery call and the proposal, the colder the lead gets.

The math: if each proposal takes 6 to 10 hours and you pursue 3 to 4 new opportunities a month, that is 18 to 40 hours, the equivalent of another half to full engagement worth of capacity consumed by pre-sales.
Bottleneck 3

Engagement reporting and KPI tracking eating into delivery time

Clients expect to see progress against the SMART goals you set in the Establish phase. Pulling that data, formatting it, and presenting it weekly or monthly is necessary but repetitive.

The math: at 3 to 4 hours per client per month across 2 to 3 active engagements, that is 6 to 12 hours a month of senior time on reporting instead of the strategic work that drives results.
